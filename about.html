About Basai

Your trusted partner for hostel management in Nepal

Welcome to Basai

Basai is Nepal's leading student hostel finder platform, connecting students with the best accommodations near colleges and universities in Kathmandu. Since our establishment in 2020, we've been dedicated to making hostel finding simple, secure, and accessible for all students.

Our platform brings together carefully selected hostels that meet our high standards for safety, cleanliness, and student-friendly facilities. Whether you're attending TU, KU, Patan Campus, or any other college, we help you find the perfect hostel near your campus.

Our Mission

To revolutionize student accommodation in Nepal by providing a seamless, transparent platform that connects students with quality hostels near their colleges while supporting local businesses and promoting affordable student living.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted student hostel platform in South Asia, known for exceptional service, innovation, and our commitment to making student living affordable and convenient.

Our Story

2020

The Beginning

Basai was founded by college students who saw the need for a reliable student hostel finder platform in Nepal. We started with just 5 hostels near major universities.

2022

Expansion

We expanded to 50+ hostels across Kathmandu and introduced our mobile-friendly platform, making booking easier than ever.

2024

Digital Payments

Integration with eSewa and Khalti made us the first hostel platform in Nepal to offer multiple digital payment options.

2026

Present Day

Today, we manage 100+ hostels, serve thousands of students annually, and continue to innovate in student accommodation.
