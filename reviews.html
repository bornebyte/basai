Amazing hostel with incredible staff!

I stayed at Thamel Heights Hostel for a week, and it exceeded all my expectations. The staff was incredibly friendly and helpful, always going out of their way to assist. The rooms were spotlessly clean, and the rooftop terrace had amazing views of the mountains. The location is perfect for exploring Kathmandu - right in the heart of Thamel but quiet enough to sleep well. Breakfast was delicious with good variety. I met so many wonderful people here. Highly recommend!
