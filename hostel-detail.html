About This Hostel

Thamel Heights Hostel is a vibrant and social hostel located in the heart of Kathmandu's tourist district. We offer a perfect blend of comfort, affordability, and community atmosphere. Our hostel features modern amenities, spacious common areas, and a rooftop terrace with stunning mountain views.

Whether you're a first-year student, a senior, or studying with friends, our friendly staff and diverse community of students will make you feel at home. We organize study groups, college events, and cultural experiences to help you make the most of your student life in Kathmandu.
