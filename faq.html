Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking and staying at our hostels

How do I book a hostel through Basai?

Booking through Basai is easy! Simply browse our hostels, select your desired dates and room type, fill in your details, and proceed to payment. You'll receive instant confirmation via email once your booking is complete.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 48 hours before check-in through your dashboard. Changes to dates or room types are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept eSewa, Khalti, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), bank transfers, and cash payment at the hostel. All online payments are secured with SSL encryption.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, we believe in transparent pricing. The total amount shown at checkout includes all taxes and service fees. There are no hidden charges.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by hostel. Most hostels allow cancellation up to 7 days before move-in for a full refund. Contact the hostel directly for their specific cancellation terms.

What is the move-in process?

Move-in times vary by hostel. Most hostels allow move-in anytime during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM). Contact the hostel in advance to arrange your move-in time. Late arrivals can usually be accommodated with prior notice.

Is WiFi included in the booking?

Yes, all our partner hostels provide free high-speed WiFi throughout the property. Password will be provided at check-in.

Are meals included in the price?

It depends on the hostel and room type you select. Many hostels include breakfast. Check the specific hostel details for meal inclusions. Additional meals can usually be purchased separately.

Do I need to print my booking confirmation?

No, you can show your booking confirmation on your mobile device. However, bringing a printed copy or having it accessible offline is recommended as a backup.

Can I bring pets to the hostel?

Most student hostels do not allow pets to maintain a clean and allergy-free environment for all residents. Service animals may be permitted with proper documentation. Check with your specific hostel.

When will I be charged for my booking?

Payment is processed immediately upon confirmation of your booking. For "Pay at Hostel" option, a deposit may be required, with the remaining amount due at check-in.

Is there parking available at the hostels?

Many of our hostels offer free parking, but availability varies by location. Urban hostels may have limited parking. Check the hostel's amenities list or contact them directly.

Can I book for a group?

Yes, we can accommodate group bookings. For groups of 10 or more, please contact us directly for special rates and arrangements. We can help coordinate multiple rooms and group activities.

What documents do I need for check-in?

You'll need a valid government-issued ID (citizenship for Nepali students, passport for international students) and your booking confirmation. Some hostels may require a security deposit at move-in.

Are towels and linens provided?

Yes, all our hostels provide clean bed linens. Towels are usually provided in private rooms, but may be available for rent in dormitories. Check your specific hostel's amenities.
